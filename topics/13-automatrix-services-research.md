## Overview

Automatrix Services Limited is an IT and digital services company headquartered in Gulshan-2, Dhaka (Grand Delvista, Level 8, Plot 1A, Road 113). It sells digital transformation to SMEs and enterprises: CRM and call-center automation (chatbots, IVR), AI/ML solutions, RFID-based warehouse management, POS systems, web/mobile development, plus BPO-style services (back office, staffing/resource augmentation, digital marketing). Industries served: BFSI, e-commerce, manufacturing, on-demand services, real estate, telecom.

⚠️ **Identity conflict — read before the interview:** the company's own [About page](https://automatrixservices.com/about-us/) claims "established 2012, publicly listed, nearly 4,000 employees," but their [LinkedIn company page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/automatrix-services-limited) says **founded 2025, 11–50 employees (only 6 listed)**. The 4,000-employee BPO description reads like borrowed or parent-company marketing copy. Most likely reality: a small, young engineering company (possibly a spin-off or sister concern of a larger BPO/contact-center operation). **Ask directly which entity you'd be employed by and the actual team size.**
